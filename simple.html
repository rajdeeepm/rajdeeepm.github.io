About

I build human-centered AI systems that blend rigorous research with practical, trustworthy user experiences. My work spans agentic LLMs, multimodal agents, reinforcement learning, and accessibility-aware interfaces, with an emphasis on reliability, evaluation, and real-world deployment.

I am currently pursuing a B.S.E. in Computer Science (Intelligent Systems) with a minor in Mathematics at the University of Michigan (GPA 3.555). Previously, I completed a B.S. in Computer Science with an AI specialization at Oakland University (Honors College, GPA 3.92).

I like building end-to-end systems: data pipelines, model training, evaluation, and the interface people actually use. I care about agent safety, interpretability, and making advanced AI accessible to diverse users.
