About

I am currently pursuing a B.S.E. in Computer Science (Intelligent Systems) with a minor in Mathematics at the University of Michigan (GPA 3.555). Previously, I completed a B.S. in Computer Science with an AI specialization at Oakland University (Honors College, GPA 3.92).

My work focuses on agentic systems that can reason, learn, and assist humans in meaningful ways. I build full stacks from data and models to evaluation and the interface people actually use.

Research Interests

  • Agentic LLMs and tool-using systems
  • Multimodal agents (audio, vision, and web interaction)
  • Reinforcement learning and decision making under uncertainty
  • Human-AI interaction, accessibility, and trustworthy AI
